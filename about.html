Who is this Sarah person? A design geek,
humanitarian, travel junkie, proponent of tea, Korean pop music and being nice to people.

A little bit weird. In a good way.

"It is a happy talent to know how to play."
- Ralph Waldo Emerson

Personal philosophy: being a successful working professional shouldn't equate to being unpleasant to be around or stepping on others to get ahead. It shouldn't mean that you never take time off, don't have any hobbies outside of work or smile at people around you everyday. It also shouldn't mean that you can't be yourself. Why? Because that's not sustainable long term for a happy life. You can get the work done on time without losing yourself.

"Be somebody who makes everybody feel like an somebody ... Be kind. Work hard. Stay humble. Smile often. Stay loyal. Keep honest. Travel when possible. Never stop learning. Be thankful always."
