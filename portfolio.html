A Few Published Projects

Duke Energy Community Redesign

This corporate communications project required a mix of information architecture, interaction design and content planning. I worked to create a fresh redesign for a client who needed a big update. The goal was to show off their wonderful work in the community in a way that was simple and easy for the client to maintain.

Transfer Money Widget (ACH)

Bank transfers to and from brokers can be confusing - customers often have difficulty understanding which way their money is going using overly complex interfaces that are poor in visual description. Using our established UI style for the LIVE platform, I worked to create a small tile widget that could be used across devices. It is simple and keeps the primary task in the forefront, while accomodating error messsages, financial rules and system restrictions in an easy-to-follow way.

Financial Binomial Probability Calculator

This was one of my most challenging projects, because the data and calculations were so complex to convey and simplify for a responsive design. The accuracy of the calculator is completely dependent on 3 main user-controlled inputs, which require stock research. Still, by using color, scalable elements, and simple copy and researching user interaction patterns and constantly iterating, I was able to design a product that scored well on a survey with some tough and incredulous customers - options traders.

Making Financial Disclaimers (More) Fun

This is a small tweak on a creative project to work with another designer on a product marketing page. We had to include FINRA disclaimers about the product, but instead of minimizing them at the bottom, I came up with the idea to use a background from the application to make the page more visual. Something required and dull became an opportunity to showcase the great design of our product in a subtle way.
